1. Overview

This page explains how users of Merge & Blast Defense, developed by Wuhan Jiangchen Technology Co., Ltd., can request deletion of data associated with their use of the game.

The game does not require users to create an account. However, the game may use third-party advertising and analytics services that process certain device, advertising, usage, and diagnostic data according to their own privacy policies.

2. How to Request Data Deletion

Email: YOUR_PRIVACY_EMAIL@example.com

3. What Data Will Be Deleted

After receiving a valid request, we will delete or anonymize data that can reasonably be associated with your request, where technically possible and required by applicable law.

Because the game does not use a player account system, some data may be stored only by third-party services or may not be directly identifiable without information such as your Advertising ID.

4. Data Handled by Third-Party Services

Merge & Blast Defense may use third-party advertising and analytics services, such as Google AdMob, Unity LevelPlay, Unity Ads, and ironSource. These services may process data according to their own privacy policies and data deletion processes.

For data collected by third-party advertising services, you may also manage or reset your Android Advertising ID in your device settings and review privacy options provided by those services.

5. Data Retention

Some data may be retained where necessary for legal compliance, security, fraud prevention, dispute resolution, platform compliance, or legitimate business purposes. Data retained for these purposes will be kept only for as long as reasonably necessary.

6. Processing Time

We will review deletion requests and respond within a reasonable period, subject to applicable law and the technical ability to identify and delete the requested data.

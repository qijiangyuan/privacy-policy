Delete your Infinite Talent: Tower RPG data

This page explains how users of Infinite Talent: Tower RPG (package name com.jcgame.tianfurpg.global) can delete locally stored game data or request deletion of other data associated with the Game. The Game is provided by jcgame.

The Game does not create a jcgame account and does not upload your saved game progress to a jcgame cloud server. You can still request deletion of data that may be processed in connection with advertising, purchases, or support.
1

Delete local game data

  1. Open Android Settings.
  2. Select Apps, then Infinite Talent: Tower RPG.
  3. Select Storage & cache.
  4. Tap Clear storage or Clear data.

You can also delete local game data by uninstalling the Game.

2

Send a deletion request

Email us with the subject Data Deletion Request – Infinite Talent: Tower RPG. State that you use Android and describe the data you want deleted.

Email a deletion request

qijiangyuan@gmail.com

Information to include

  • The Game name: Infinite Talent: Tower RPG;
  • Your platform: Android;
  • The type of request, such as deleting advertising-related or purchase-related data;
  • A device or advertising identifier only if we later ask for it to locate relevant records.

Do not email us your password, full payment-card number, bank information, or identity documents. We may need to verify that a request relates to you before acting on it. We normally respond within 30 days, or within the period required by applicable law.

Data deleted and data retained

Data deleted

  • Clearing app data or uninstalling deletes locally stored progress, settings, cached subscription-entitlement status, and local ad-frequency counters from that device.
  • Following a verified request, we will delete or anonymize personal information under our control that is not required to be retained.
  • Where applicable, we will help direct the request to a relevant service provider or explain how to use that provider’s deletion controls.

Data that may be retained

  • The Game has no jcgame account or server-side cloud save to delete.
  • Google Play and Unity may retain data under their own policies, including transaction records required for accounting, fraud prevention, security, dispute resolution, or legal compliance.
  • We may retain deletion-request correspondence for up to 90 days after closing the request to document its completion, unless a longer period is required by law or for a legal claim.
  • Aggregated or anonymized information that can no longer identify you may be retained.

Subscriptions and third-party data

Deleting game data does not cancel an active Google Play subscription. Manage or cancel subscriptions separately in Google Play before deleting data. Payment and transaction data controlled by Google must be managed through your Google account or Google support.

  • Manage Google Play subscriptions
  • Manage Google account activity
  • Unity game-player privacy controls

Within a Unity advertisement, you may also open the Data Privacy icon to view available privacy options and request deletion of historical Unity Ads data associated with that app and device.

删除《Infinite Talent: Tower RPG》相关数据

  1. 打开 Android 设置；

2

发送删除请求

请以 Data Deletion Request – Infinite Talent: Tower RPG 为主题向我们发送电子邮件，说明您使用 Android，并描述希望删除的数据。

发送删除请求邮件

qijiangyuan@gmail.com

邮件中需要提供的信息

  • 游戏名称：Infinite Talent: Tower RPG；
  • 使用平台：Android；
  • 请求类型，例如删除广告相关或购买相关数据；
  • 仅在我们随后要求用于查找相关记录时，提供设备或广告标识符。

请勿通过邮件发送密码、完整支付卡号、银行信息或身份证件。处理请求前，我们可能 需要核实该请求确实与您有关。我们通常会在 30 天内或适用法律规定的期限内回复。

删除和保留的数据

将删除的数据

  • 收到并核实请求后，我们会删除或匿名化由我们控制且无须继续保留的个人信息；
  • 在适用情况下，我们会协助将请求转交给相关服务商，或说明如何使用该服务商的删除功能。

Privacy Policy for Haven Afloat: Sea Survival

The Game does not require an account and does not ask you to provide your name, postal address, phone number, or email address to play. Game progress and settings are stored locally on your device.
No game account No registration or profile is required.
Local game saves Progress is kept on your device, not on our servers.
Ads and purchases Third-party services process limited data to provide these features.

1. Information We Process

Information stored locally

The Game stores game progress, preferences, subscription-entitlement status, and rewarded-ad frequency information on your device. We do not operate a server that receives your saved game progress.

Advertising information

The Game uses Unity LevelPlay mediation and Unity Ads to provide rewarded advertisements. These providers may collect or receive:

  • Approximate location inferred from your IP address;
  • Device or other identifiers, including an advertising identifier where available;
  • Ad interactions, such as ad requests, impressions, clicks, and rewarded-ad completion;
  • Technical and diagnostic information, such as device type, operating system, app version, language, network information, and errors.

Purchases and subscriptions

If you buy a subscription, Google Play processes the payment. The Game receives product, transaction, purchase-state, and entitlement information needed to complete the purchase, restore it, and provide subscription benefits. We do not receive or store your full payment-card or bank-account details.

Unity In-App Purchasing may also process the email address associated with the store transaction, user and purchase identifiers, purchase history, approximate location, and technical information such as crash logs, diagnostics, performance data, and device identifiers for app functionality and analytics.

Information we do not request

The Game does not request precise location, contacts, photos, videos, audio recordings, camera access, microphone access, health information, or files and documents from your device for gameplay.

2. How and Why Information Is Used

  • To operate the Game, save your progress locally, and remember your preferences;
  • To load, display, measure, and limit the frequency of rewarded advertisements;
  • To process subscriptions, confirm entitlements, and restore purchases;
  • To diagnose errors, maintain reliability, secure the services, and prevent fraud or abuse;
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce applicable terms.

Where applicable law requires a legal basis, processing may be based on your consent (for example, certain personalized advertising), performance of a contract, compliance with law, or legitimate interests such as service security, fraud prevention, and reliability. You may withdraw consent through available consent controls without affecting processing that occurred before withdrawal.

3. Third-Party Services and Sharing

Data may be transmitted directly from your device to the providers below. They process information under their own terms and privacy policies. The particular provider used may depend on your platform, region, device, and ad availability.

  • Unity LevelPlay and Unity Ads privacy policy
  • Google privacy policy (Google Play and Google Play Billing)

We do not sell your personal information for money. Advertising providers may process or share identifiers and ad-interaction information for advertising, measurement, fraud prevention, security, and compliance. Some privacy laws may define certain personalized-advertising disclosures as “sharing” or “targeted advertising.” You can use the choices described below where applicable.

We may also disclose information if required by law, to protect users and the Game, or as part of a business transfer where legally permitted and subject to appropriate notice.

4. Your Privacy Choices and Rights

  • You can manage ad-personalization and reset or delete the advertising identifier through your Android privacy or ads settings.
  • You can use any privacy or consent controls presented by the Game or its advertising providers.
  • You can manage or cancel subscriptions in Google Play.
  • You can delete locally stored game progress by clearing the Game’s app data or uninstalling the Game.
  • You can follow our Data Deletion Request instructions to request deletion of other data.

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict personal information, object to or opt out of certain processing, and withdraw consent. Contact us to make a request. Because the Game does not use a jcgame account or cloud save, we may not be able to identify data held by an advertising or store provider; requests concerning that data may need to be made directly to the relevant provider.

5. Retention and Deletion

Local game data remains on your device until you clear the Game’s app data or uninstall it. We retain any information we receive only for as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this Policy, including legal, accounting, security, and fraud-prevention needs. Third-party providers retain and delete data according to their own policies. You may contact us to request deletion of any personal information that we control, subject to applicable legal exceptions.

We and our service providers use reasonable technical and organizational measures designed to protect information, including encrypted transmission where supported.
